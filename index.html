Neighborhood Highlights

One of Tampa's most vibrant and walkable neighborhoods. Known for historic charm, tree-lined streets, diverse community, and locally-owned businesses.

✓ Highly Walkable: Most amenities within 5-10 min walk/bike

✓ Strong Community: Active FB groups, monthly meetings, regular events

✓ Local Focus: Independent cafés, shops, restaurants, distilleries

✓ Safe Neighborhood: Low-medium crime, active watch program

Safety Tips for Residents

Home Security: Lock doors and windows. Use security lights and cameras. Get to know neighbors and look out for each other.

Vehicle Safety: Don't leave valuables in cars. Lock doors immediately. Park in well-lit areas. Report suspicious activity.

Personal Safety: Walk in groups at night. Stay aware of surroundings. Report street light outages. Share location with trusted friends.
