❤️ A Place Where Culture, Art & Community Thrive

Ybor Heights is Tampa's most vibrant and historic neighborhood! We're known for our colorful streets, iconic cigar heritage, thriving arts scene, and neighbors who celebrate diversity and creativity.

✓ Historic & Artistic: Vibrant murals, galleries, studios, and cultural landmarks around every corner

✓ Diverse Food Scene: Cuban, Spanish, Latin, and international restaurants that reflect our rich heritage

✓ Walkable & Vibrant: 7th Avenue packed with shops, bars, galleries, and local businesses buzzing with life

✓ Events & Nightlife: Year-round festivals, live music, cultural celebrations, and a thriving nightlife scene

🤝 Get Involved in Ybor Heights!

🎭 Explore Our Culture: Visit galleries, cigar shops, museums, and discover our rich historical heritage

🎵 Experience Live Music & Art: From street performers on 7th Avenue to gallery openings and festivals—Ybor is always alive with creativity

🍽️ Support Local Businesses: Eat at authentic Cuban and Latin restaurants, shop at local shops, and help our neighborhood thrive

🌟 Join Community Groups: Participate in Ybor Heights events, festivals, and neighborhood meetings. Help keep Ybor vibrant!

📰 Community Updates & News

April 2024

Increased police patrols on Florida Ave following community request. Two vehicle break-ins reported—residents advised to secure valuables and avoid leaving items in cars.

March 2024

Neighborhood Watch Zoom meeting hosted 87 residents discussing safety initiatives. New street light installed at Central Ave & Seminole.

💡 Simple Ways to Stay Safe (and Help Neighbors Stay Safe)

🏠 At Home: Lock doors and windows, use porch lights, and get to know your neighbors. Neighbors who know each other look out for each other!

🚗 With Your Car: Don't leave valuables visible, park in lit areas, and lock immediately. If you see something suspicious, give police a call.

🌙 Out & About: Walk with friends when possible, stay aware, and don't hesitate to ask neighbors for a buddy. We're here to support each other!
